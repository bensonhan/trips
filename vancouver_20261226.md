# Vancouver, BC: The Winter Edition Travel Guide

### Vancouver

**Activities and Attractions**

* **Stanley Park & Seawall:** Bike or walk the 9 km waterfront loop; see the Totem Poles and Lions Gate Bridge.
* **Bright Nights (seasonal):** The Bright Nights Christmas Train runs in Stanley Park during December.
* **Vancouver Aquarium:** Located in Stanley Park; a reliable indoor option for rainy days.
* **Science World:** Interactive science exhibits in an iconic geodesic dome; includes an OMNIMAX theatre.
* **Vancouver Art Gallery:** Features major international exhibitions and a significant collection of regional and Indigenous art.
* **FlyOver Canada:** A flight simulation ride at Canada Place; often features a seasonal "Soar with Santa" experience.
* **Vancouver Canucks (NHL Hockey):** Professional hockey games held at Rogers Arena throughout the winter season.
* **Granville Island Public Market:** Explore food stalls, artisan shops, the Net Loft, and live performances at The Improv Centre.
* **Gastown:** Historic district featuring boutiques and the famous Steam Clock.
* **Bill Reid Gallery of Northwest Coast Art:** Focused gallery celebrating Haida master artist Bill Reid and contemporary Indigenous traditions.
* **Robson Square Ice Rink:** Central outdoor skating rink with rentals available.
* **Vancouver Christmas Market:** Located at Jack Poole Plaza; features mulled wine, artisan stalls, and a carousel.
* **VanDusen Festival of Lights:** Over a million lights displayed at the VanDusen Botanical Garden.
* **Museum of Anthropology (MOA):** Located at UBC; features world-class Indigenous art.
* **Beaty Biodiversity Museum:** Located at UBC; features a blue whale skeleton and extensive biological collections.
* **Bloedel Conservatory:** An indoor tropical conservatory located at the peak of Queen Elizabeth Park.
* **Kitsilano Beach & English Bay:** Ideal for sunset views and seaside walks.
* **Vancouver Public Library (Central Branch):** Colosseum-inspired architecture with an indoor public space and rooftop garden.

**Dining**

* **Fine Dining:** L'Abattoir (Gastown), St. Lawrence (Gastown), AnnaLena (Kitsilano), Miku (Downtown).
* **Casual & Brunch:** Medina Cafe (Downtown), Nelson the Seagull (Gastown), Their There (Kitsilano).
* **Ramen & Izakaya:** Ramen Danbo (Downtown), Maruhachi Ramen (Downtown), Guu with Garlic (West End).
* **Vegetarian & Farm-to-Table:** The Acorn (Main St), Burdock & Co (Main St).
* **Drinks:** The Diamond (Gastown), Brassneck Brewery (Mount Pleasant), Sing Sing Beer Bar (Main St).

### North Vancouver

**Activities and Attractions**

* **Capilano Suspension Bridge Park:** Features the Treetop Adventure and Canyon Lights seasonal display.
* **Grouse Mountain:** Offers skiing, snowboarding, the Skyride, and Peak of Christmas events.
* **The Shipyards & Outdoor Skating Plaza:** Waterfront area in Lower Lonsdale with a large skating rink and seasonal markets.
* **The Polygon Gallery:** Contemporary photography and media gallery located near the SeaBus terminal.
* **Lynn Canyon Park:** A free alternative to Capilano with a suspension bridge and forest trails.
* **Mt Seymour:** Family-friendly mountain offering skiing, snowshoeing, and tobogganing.
* **Deep Cove:** A scenic village offering the Quarry Rock hike.

**Dining**

* **West Coast & View-Focused:** The Observatory (Grouse Mountain), Pier 7 Restaurant + Bar (Shipyards), Fishworks (Lower Lonsdale).
* **Italian:** Farina a Legna (Lower Lonsdale), Nook (Shipyards), Nicli Antica Pizzeria (Edgemont Village).
* **Casual & Food Halls:** Lonsdale Quay Market, Cream Pony (donuts and fried chicken), Arms Reach Bistro (Deep Cove).
* **International:** Maru Korean Bistro (Lower Lonsdale), Hachiro Ramen Bar (Central Lonsdale), Vinnie's (Mexican/Casual).
* **Breweries & Pubs:** Beere Brewing Co, House of Funk Brewing, North Point Brewing (all located in the Shipyards Brewery District).

### Richmond

**Activities and Attractions**

* **Steveston Village:** Historic fishing village with shops and a waterfront boardwalk.

**Dining**

* **Dim Sum:** Fisherman's Terrace, Chef Tony Seafood Restaurant.
* **Cantonese & BBQ:** The Jade Seafood Restaurant, HK BBQ Master.
* **Specialty & Regional:** Dinesty Dumpling House (Xiao Long Bao), Fish Eater (Szechuan), Su Hang Restaurant (Shanghainese).
* **Hot Pot:** Haidilao, The Dolar Shop.

### Regional Day Trips

* **Squamish & Whistler:** Take the Sea-to-Sky Highway for the Sea-to-Sky Gondola, Shannon Falls, and world-class skiing at Whistler Blackcomb.
* **Cypress Mountain:** Offers the largest skiable terrain in the local area, including cross-country skiing and tubing.
* **Burnaby Village Museum:** Features a Heritage Christmas display with traditional lights and a vintage carousel.
* **Victoria:** The provincial capital on Vancouver Island; features the Inner Harbour and Butchart Gardens.
* **Bowen Island:** A short ferry ride from Horseshoe Bay for hiking and quiet seaside cafes.
